Learn to save lives – don’t ignore the power of first aid!

Art Meets AI 2.0: The Next Act

Scroll to read

First Aid – Don’t Ignore! is an international youth exchange that brings together young people from Poland, Romania, and Hungary to explore the vital topic of first aid through creative, educational, and practical activities. In a world increasingly affected by crises, conflicts, and disasters, the project aims to empower youth with the knowledge and confidence to respond effectively in emergencies while promoting solidarity, civic responsibility, and the spirit of helping others.

During the exchange, participants will engage in non-formal learning sessions to gain both theoretical and practical understanding of first aid. They will explore key concepts such as the “golden hour,” “platinum minutes,” and the “chain of survival,” while practicing how to respond in real-life scenarios, including emergencies involving natural disasters, refugees, and victims of violence. Interactive workshops, simulations, and teamwork will help them develop essential skills for handling critical situations and providing support to those in need, especially within vulnerable communities.


Creative and Social Impact

Beyond skill-building, the project will encourage creativity and community engagement. Participants will design a digital first aid app with accessible tips, record an educational podcast focusing on first aid in crises such as wars and disasters, and stage a theatrical performance addressing violence against ethnic minorities. These activities will not only spread awareness but also serve as tools for social change and education at local and European levels. Through their collective effort, participants will strengthen their sense of empathy, cooperation, and civic engagement, embodying the Erasmus+ values of inclusion, participation, and lifelong learning.



Join Us if You’re Interested!

If you’re passionate about helping others, learning practical life-saving skills, and making a difference in your community, First Aid – Don’t Ignore! is the perfect opportunity to take action. This international youth exchange welcomes young people eager to explore the world of first aid, gain hands-on experience through engaging workshops, and connect with peers from across Europe. Whether you dream of becoming a healthcare professional, want to be prepared in times of crisis, or simply wish to contribute to a safer, more caring society, join us on this meaningful journey, where knowledge empowers action and compassion saves lives.
