About Us

What we do

Empowering young people through creativity, technology, and sustainability.
Our projects transform ideas into impactful actions, fostering inclusion, environmental awareness, and ethical innovation.

  • We design international programs that equip young people with skills in leadership, critical thinking, and cultural understanding through hands-on, creative experiences.

  • Our initiatives turn environmental challenges into opportunities for change—through art installations, upcycled fashion, and community-driven eco-projects.

  • We help youth explore the potential of artificial intelligence responsibly, combining cutting-edge AI powered tools, 3D printing and coding, with creative practices for theater, design, and media production.

  • From immersive cultural evenings to global awareness campaigns, we use art, music, and storytelling to connect communities and promote European values.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

We organize international youth projects that combine art, technology, and environmental action. Our programs focus on non-formal education, intercultural exchange, and skill-building in areas like creativity, leadership, and digital literacy.

Our projects are open to young people aged 18–30 from Europe, especially those with fewer opportunities or coming from disadvantaged backgrounds. We ensure diversity and inclusion in every program.

Yes! Our initiatives are co-funded by the Erasmus+ program, which covers accommodation, food, activities, and even travel (within the set Erasmus+ limits).
