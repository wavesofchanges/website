Art Meets AI 2.0: The Next Act

Scroll to read

Waves of Change is launching Art Meets AI 2.0: The Next Act, the continuation of last year’s acclaimed Erasmus+ project Art Meets AI: Exploring New Forms of Performance. This new edition explores how artificial intelligence has advanced in just one year and how these changes are transforming artistic creation. It challenges young people to use technology creatively, responsibly, and ethically.

The project gathers young artists, performers, and innovators from across Europe to experiment with the newest AI tools in the performing arts. We will explore voice generation, real-time scriptwriting, and visual creation, learning how to blend digital innovation with human imagination. Our work will culminate in a live AI-enhanced theatre performance in Krosno, Poland, attended by over 1,000 students, teachers, and locals, demonstrating the evolving relationship between creativity and technology.


International Collaboration Across Borders

Participants of Art Meets AI 2.0 come from Poland, Bulgaria, Türkiye, and Moldova, uniting diverse talents and perspectives in one creative journey. Each country brings its own artistic identity, cultural depth, and vision for the future, enriching the collaborative process. Together, these young creators will explore how technology can connect people across borders, inspiring a shared European dialogue on creativity, ethics, and innovation.


Learning Beyond the Stage

Alongside artistic production, Art Meets AI 2.0 emphasizes learning, reflection, and collaboration. Through workshops, discussions, and cultural evenings, participants will explore AI’s impact on ethics, privacy, and society while d eveloping leadership, teamwork, and digital literacy. The experience will help them overcome stage fright, strengthen communication, and engage with complex global questions through creativity.


Join Us if You’re Interested!

If you’re passionate about creativity, technology, and international collaboration, Art Meets AI 2.0: The Next Act is the perfect opportunity to get involved. We welcome young people eager to explore the intersection of art and artificial intelligence, learn through hands-on experience, and share ideas with peers from across Europe. Whether you’re an artist, performer, or simply curious about the future of AI, join us on this journey of innovation and discovery—where imagination meets technology and creativity knows no limits.
